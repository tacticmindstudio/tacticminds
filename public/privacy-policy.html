Privacy Policy

Studio: Tactic Minds Studio

Applicable Apps: Water Sort Puzzle, One Line Puzzle, Tic Tac Toe King, Wood Block Blast Puzzle, Retro Racer 2D, Brick Breaker, and all related services.

Effective Date: July 2026

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications, games, and website services (herein collectively referred to as "the Applications" or "Services") created and published by Tactic Minds Studio (herein referred to as "the Developer," "we," "us," or "our").

We are dedicated to safeguarding and respecting your privacy. This document outlines what data is automatically collected when using our games, how it is managed, and the trusted third-party SDKs integrated to support gameplay performance, offline analytics, and ad delivery.

1. Information We Do Not Collect (Personal Data)

Strict Offline & Privacy First Design: None of our Applications require user registration, log-ins, or player accounts. We do not solicit, demand, or collect personal identifiers, including but not limited to your:

  • Real name or physical street address
  • Telephone numbers or direct contact profiles
  • Email addresses (unless you proactively send us feedback or a support ticket)
  • Precise GPS coordinates

2. Automatic Non-Personal Information Collection

To help maintain and improve game optimization, diagnose crash logs, and deliver advertisements, our games integrate reputable third-party software development kits (SDKs). These third-party services may collect non-personal data automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID, GAID, or iOS IDFA)
  • System and hardware metadata (device manufacturer, model, operating system, and language settings)
  • Game telemetry (levels finished, gameplay duration, score states, and active session histories)
  • IP address and coarse geolocation (restricted to country or city levels for regional ad localization)

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising Networks

To keep our Applications available for free, we show ads and analyze basic gameplay performance using industry-standard platforms. These third-party services operate according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google AdMob: Serves high-quality banners, interstitials, and rewarded videos to keep our applications free. Google uses advertising identifiers to customize non-personalized and personalized advertisements and control ad limits. View AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics & Firebase Analytics: Helps us understand level bottlenecks, analyze user retention, receive automatic crash reports, and optimize loading times. View Google Analytics / Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: Facilitates standard Android app execution, updates, platform authentication, and Google Play Achievements/Leaderboards. View Google Play Services Privacy Policy

4. Children’s Privacy Statement

Our puzzle and arcade games offer healthy, brain-sharpening entertainment designed for players of all ages. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and equivalent global regulations. We do not knowingly solicit, request, or gather personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately, and we will promptly erase such records.

5. Data Security Practices

We implement appropriate industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect the non-personal analytics and advertising metadata processed in our services. Since we do not maintain personal databases, your real-world identity is fully secure.
